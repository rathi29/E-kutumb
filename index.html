कुटुम्ब

Born in 1898 at Bareli, Ram Kishanji Rathi (RKR) was the eldest son of Sukh Deoji Rathi. Bareli is presently a small town, about 120 kms from Bhopal on National Highway-12. RKR had 8 siblings, six brothers being Narsingdas, Tulsidas, Prabhulal, Lakhmichand & Purushottamdas and two sisters Kesar Devi & Chandra Kala. Early years of RKR were spent between Phalodi & Bareli, until he was married to Sugni Devi, a girl from Bhattar Family of Phalodi. The couple had 8 sons & 3 daughters. He joined father's business of Ghee & Grain. But business did not suit his ethics as it involved speculations. So he decided to join service in a Marwari group (Bansilal Abhirchand-HQ at Hinganghat) & took charge of operations (mainly agriculture) around Vidisha.. He shifted family from Bareli to Goberela, a small village near district town Vidisha.
